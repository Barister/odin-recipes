Navy Bean Soup

Description

This hearty navy bean soup will warm you up on a cold night.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 (16 ounce) package dried navy beans
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • ½ pound chopped ham
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine water, beans, tomatoes, ham, onion, celery, Worcestershire sauce, minced garlic, bouillon, parsley, garlic powder, and bay leaf in a stock pot; bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for two hours.
  2. Add 3 cups of water; stir in salt and pepper. Simmer for an additional two hours. Discard bay leaf before serving.
